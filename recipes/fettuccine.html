Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

This Fettuccine is absolutely to die for and is a staple dish in my kitchen. I have developed it, again, with prep time and simplicity in mind.

This recipe only uses five simple ingredients to create an authentic and tasty Alfredo for you and your guests

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine Noodles
  • Pasta Water
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper
  • (Optionally) Cream
